§ 04 Avatar & Favicon

One letter, one period.

Instrument Serif italic "S" with the red period, locked in a square or circle. Works at 40×40 px. The period carries the .AI signal so the mark never feels half-finished.

§ 05 Clear Space

Give it air.

Minimum clear space on all sides = the height of the capital "S" in the wordmark. Don't let anything — copy, buttons, photos — enter the red "x" zone.

§ 06 Misuse · Don'ts

Things that kill the brand.

Three quick rules. If a variation drifts into any of these, it's not the Skillies.AI mark anymore.

SKILLIES.AI
No purple/blue/SaaS gradients. The palette is cream, charcoal, red — plus sage / forest / gold.
skillies.ai
No substituted fonts. Inter Black + Instrument Serif italic only. No Comic Sans, Poppins, Roboto.
S K I L L I E S. A I
No wide tracking. The wordmark is tight (−0.055em). Never spaced-out, never lowercase only.
