Rules

  1. Always four parts — red rule, label, fade spacer, caption. Never drop the caption.
  2. Caption is never a sentence unless it's a punchy one. Periods optional.
  3. Tracking is 0.32em. Less reads sloppy, more reads affected.
  4. No emoji. No icons. The § is the only glyph allowed.
  5. Don't animate. It's set type. Magazines don't fade in.
