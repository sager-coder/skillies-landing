Instrument Serif · the italic pullout

Use Instrument Serif italic only as an accent inside an otherwise bold-sans heading, or as a pullquote inside an answer. Never for whole paragraphs. The contrast is the point.

I could publish more books and earn more. I'm choosing not to.

The sans line anchors the sentence. The italic serif is the emotional beat — only ever one line per heading.
